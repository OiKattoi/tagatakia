Ta Gatakia (https://tagatakia.com/) and Ta Kattouthkia (https://takattouthkia.com/) are upcoming projects by the “Cats of Cyprus” (https://oikattoi.com/ and https://oikattes.com/) creators.

Ta Gatakia is an interim project to the series, to be considered a “special event” launch on the Ethereum blockchain, with an A.I. generated series of images of teenage cats acting cool.

“Ta Kattouthkia” (v3) is a series successor, the sequel to “Oi Kattes” (v2), which in turn is a sequel to “Oi Kattoi” (v1). Ta Kattouthkia is to be considered version 3 of the series and the final version, which will close the project with another Solana blockchain-based NFT that will be supported by a 2d video game (playable on iOS and Android) with benefits to the NFT holders of v1 and v2.

All proceeds from all four projects are donated to programs and individuals in Cyprus to help raise awareness of the problems with stray and feral cats in Cyprus, as well as to provide for their well-being by providing funds to support TNR programs, emergency care, food, and shelter.

Ta Gatakia seeks to raise funds to support the development of the video game intended for v3.

Using DALL-E 2, each image of Ta Gatakia will be generated, as a real photo of cats wearing and posing for portrait photos.

All members of the existing discord server https://discord.gg/tagatakia will be whitelisted and granted special rewards. The rewards require attendance or specific actions to unlock reward tiers, as follows:

  • Online on the 13th of November, at a specific time, to receive 1 level
  • Online on the 20th of November, at a specific time, to receive 1 level
  • Owner of at least 1 of v1, to receive 10 levels
  • Owner of at least 10 of v1, to receive 100 levels
  • Owner of at least 1 of v2, to receive 20 levels
  • Owner of at least 10 of v2, to receive 200 levels
  • Invited up to 10 members, post reset (reset will be on the 13th of November), to receive 1 level per invite

The Ta Gatakia mint date will be the 1st of December and the 4th of December. More details are to be determined. The likely price of the mint will be 0.1 ETH, and the discounts will be provided to members who have acquired at least 2 levels, as per the following:

  • 1 level, no discount
  • 2 levels, 10% discount
  • 3-12 levels, 11-20% discount
  • 39-99 levels, 30% discount
  • 100+, 50% discount

Levels will be summed up and granted as a final reward on the day of the mint, hours before the pre-sale.

All whitelisted members can access the sale early, on a pre-sale secret mint website.

Bonus rewards will be given to members who have achieved certain ranks and other goals, as part of the giveaways that will occur weekly and daily. Some of these include giveaway v2 “Oi Kattes” which will heavily contribute to the levels above.

All members can access the sale on the public website, https://tagatakia.com when launched and on OpenSea as a secondary when the listing is completed.

The project and the project owners will not promise any financial utilities, such as buyback or floor sweeps, however, any member is free to act at their own discretion, including the owners.

The only utility of this project is to promote the development of v3 and provide benefits to holders within the v3 project, which intends to provide to charity but also provide a stackable token that will be launched in the future.

As a backward utility, it provides large benefits to holders of v1 and v2, community supporters, and backers of the series.

Ta Gatakia is a limited collection of 100 cute and cool realistic cats, that appear to be taken as portrait photos.
